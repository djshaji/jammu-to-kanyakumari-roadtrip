## Day 1 — Jan 1: Kanyakumari → Thiruvananthapuram (Trivandrum)
- Approx. 90 km
- Short drive; visit Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Kovalam beach.
- Overnight: Trivandrum

## Day 2 — Jan 2: Trivandrum → Kochi (via Kollam/Alleppey)
- Approx. 210 km
- Scenic coastal drive, optional houseboat/backwaters in Alleppey.
- Overnight: Kochi

## Day 3 — Jan 3: Kochi → Kozhikode (Malabar coast)
- Approx. 190 km
- Morning sightseeing in Fort Kochi, then drive to Kozhikode.
- Overnight: Kozhikode

## Day 4 — Jan 4: Kozhikode → Mangaluru
- Approx. 230 km
- Coastal drive, enjoy Malabar cuisine.
- Overnight: Mangaluru

## Day 5 — Jan 5: Mangaluru → Goa (via Karwar)
- Approx. 360 km
- Scenic drive along the coast, enter Goa by evening.
- Overnight: Goa

## Day 6 — Jan 6: Goa (Rest/Sightseeing)
- Explore North and South Goa beaches, forts, churches.
- Overnight: Goa

## Day 7 — Jan 7: Goa → Mumbai
- Approx. 590 km
- Early start, drive up the Konkan coast, bypassing Ratnagiri.
- Overnight: Mumbai

## Day 8 — Jan 8: Mumbai → Jaipur/Ajmer corridor
- Approx. 1,150 km (Mumbai to Jaipur), can split with a stop at Vadodara/Udaipur if needed
- Long drive north, aim to be within 500–300 km of Delhi.
- Overnight: Jaipur/Ajmer

## Day 9 — Jan 9: Jaipur/Ajmer → Delhi
- Approx. 300–400 km
- Final leg, arrive in Delhi by evening.

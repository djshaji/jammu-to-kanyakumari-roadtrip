# Maruti DZire 2017 Model

## Vehicle Specifications
- **Model**: Maruti Suzuki DZire (2017)
- **Engine**: 1.3L DDiS 200 Diesel / 1.2L K-Series Petrol
- **Fuel Tank Capacity**: 43 liters
- **Mileage**: 
  - Diesel: ~24-28 km/l (highway)
  - Petrol: ~20-22 km/l (highway)
- **Ground Clearance**: 163mm
- **Seating**: 5 passengers
- **Boot Space**: 378 liters

## DZire-Specific Tips for Long Distance
- **Fuel Efficiency**: Maintain 80-90 km/h for optimal mileage
- **AC Usage**: Use recirculation mode on highways to reduce load
- **Luggage**: Distribute weight evenly; avoid roof loading
- **Driving**: Smooth acceleration/braking for better fuel economy

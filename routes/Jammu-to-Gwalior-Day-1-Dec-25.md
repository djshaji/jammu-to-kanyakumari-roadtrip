## Jammu to Gwalior (Day 1: Dec 25)
- **Start Location:** Jammu
- **End Location:** Gwalior, MP
- **Approx. Distance:** ≈850–900 km
- **Key Stops/Notes:** VERY LONG DRIVE. Start before dawn. Use the main highways (NH 44 route). Minimal stopping.
- Tourist Stop: Gwalior Fort (Evening/Next Morning).

### Fastest Route (NH/Toll)
- Jammu → Pathankot → Jalandhar → Ludhiana → Ambala → Karnal → Panipat via NH 44 (toll).
- At Panipat, take the Eastern Peripheral Expressway (EPE/NE-2) towards Palwal/Noida to bypass Delhi (toll).
- Connect to Noida–Greater Noida Expressway → Yamuna Expressway (YEW) to Agra (access-controlled, toll).
- From Agra, continue to Gwalior via NH 44/AH43 (toll).

**Key interchanges:**
- Panipat: NH 44 → EPE (NE-2) southbound.
- Greater Noida: Noida–Greater Noida Expwy → Yamuna Expwy (YEW).
- Agra: YEW terminus → NH 44 towards Gwalior.

**Estimates:**
- Time (moving): 12–14 hrs; with breaks: 14–16 hrs.
- Tolls (SUV approx.): ₹1,800–₹2,400 (varies by class/time).

**Notes:**
- Keep FASTag funded; speed limits on YEW strictly enforced.
- Prefer early morning transit to clear Punjab–Haryana corridors and EPE.

#### Historical Places & Tourist Attractions (with Detour Distances)

- **Jammu:** Raghunath Temple, Bahu Fort, Mubarak Mandi Palace *(in city)*
- **Pathankot:** Mukteshwar Temple (~8 km from NH 44), Ranjit Sagar Dam (~15 km from NH 44)
- **Jalandhar:** Devi Talab Mandir *(in city, ~5 km from NH 44)*
- **Ludhiana:** Lodhi Fort *(in city, ~6 km from NH 44)*
- **Ambala:** Gurudwara Manji Sahib *(in city, ~4 km from NH 44)*
- **Karnal:** Karna Lake *(adjacent to NH 44)*
- **Panipat:** Panipat Museum, Kabuli Bagh Mosque, Tomb of Ibrahim Lodi *(all within ~5 km of NH 44)*
- **Greater Noida/YEW:** Buddh International Circuit (~10 km from YEW), Surajpur Bird Sanctuary (~12 km from YEW)
- **Agra:** Taj Mahal (~7 km from NH 44/YEW terminus), Agra Fort (~6 km from NH 44/YEW terminus), Itmad-ud-Daulah’s Tomb (~8 km from NH 44/YEW terminus), Mehtab Bagh (~9 km from NH 44/YEW terminus)
- **Gwalior:** Gwalior Fort, Jai Vilas Palace, Sas Bahu Temples, Teli Ka Mandir *(all within ~7 km of NH 44)*

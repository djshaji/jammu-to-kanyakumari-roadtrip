## Kozhikode to Mangaluru (Day 11: Jan 4)
- **Start Location:** Kozhikode (Calicut), KL
- **End Location:** Mangaluru, KA
- **Approx. Distance:** ≈230 km
- **Key Stops/Notes:** Scenic coastal drive along NH66. Plan for 5–6 hours with stops.

---
### Fastest Route (National Highways)
**Route:**
Kozhikode → Kannur (NH66) → Kasaragod (NH66) → Mangaluru (NH66)

**Major Highway:** NH66 (entire stretch)

**Toll Plazas (approximate):**
- Kozhikode exit toll (NH66)
- Kannur Toll Plaza (NH66)
- Kasaragod Toll Plaza (NH66)
- Mangaluru entry toll (NH66)

**Notes:**
This route is the fastest and most reliable, with good road conditions and facilities. Expect tolls at major highway sections. FASTag recommended.

---
### Tourist Attractions & Landmarks (En-route/Minor Detour)

1. **Kozhikode Beach** (Kozhikode) – Popular city beach. (0 km detour)
2. **Kannur Fort (St. Angelo's Fort)** (Kannur) – Historic fort. (~4 km detour from NH66)
3. **Payyambalam Beach** (Kannur) – Scenic beach. (0 km detour)
4. **Muzhappilangad Drive-in Beach** (near Kannur) – India's longest drive-in beach. (~7 km detour from NH66)
5. **Bekal Fort** (near Kasaragod) – Iconic coastal fort. (~13 km detour from NH66)
6. **Kappil Beach** (near Kasaragod) – Quiet beach. (~10 km detour from NH66)
7. **Tannirbhavi Beach** (Mangaluru) – Popular beach. (~8 km detour from NH66)
8. **St. Aloysius Chapel** (Mangaluru) – Famous for its frescoes. (0 km detour)
9. **Mangaladevi Temple** (Mangaluru) – Ancient temple. (0 km detour)

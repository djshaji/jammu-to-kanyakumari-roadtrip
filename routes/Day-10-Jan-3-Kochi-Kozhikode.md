## Kochi to Kozhikode (Day 10: Jan 3)
- **Start Location:** Kochi, KL
- **End Location:** Kozhikode (Calicut), KL
- **Approx. Distance:** ≈190 km
- **Key Stops/Notes:** Scenic coastal drive along NH66. Plan for 4–5 hours with stops.

---
### Fastest Route (National Highways)
**Route:**
Kochi → Thrissur (NH66) → Guruvayur (NH66) → Kunnamkulam (NH66) → Tirur (NH66) → Kozhikode (NH66)

**Major Highway:** NH66 (entire stretch)

**Toll Plazas (approximate):**
- Kochi exit toll (NH66)
- Thrissur Toll Plaza (NH66)
- Kozhikode entry toll (NH66)

**Notes:**
This route is the fastest and most reliable, with good road conditions and facilities. Expect tolls at major highway sections. FASTag recommended.

---
### Tourist Attractions & Landmarks (En-route/Minor Detour)

1. **Athirappilly Falls** (near Thrissur) – Famous waterfall. (~35 km detour from NH66 at Thrissur)
2. **Vadakkunnathan Temple** (Thrissur) – Ancient Shiva temple. (0 km detour)
3. **Guruvayur Temple** (Guruvayur) – Major Krishna temple. (~6 km detour from NH66)
4. **Punnathur Kotta Elephant Sanctuary** (near Guruvayur) – Elephant camp. (~8 km detour from NH66)
5. **Kozhikode Beach** (Kozhikode) – Popular city beach. (0 km detour)
6. **Mananchira Square** (Kozhikode) – Historic park and lake. (0 km detour)
7. **Kappad Beach** (near Kozhikode) – Historic landing site of Vasco da Gama. (~18 km detour from NH66)
8. **Beypore Port & Beach** (near Kozhikode) – Ancient port, boat building. (~10 km detour from NH66)

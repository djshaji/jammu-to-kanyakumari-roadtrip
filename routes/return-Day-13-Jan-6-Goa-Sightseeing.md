### Suggested Sightseeing Route
**North Goa:**
1. **Fort Aguada** – Historic fort with sea views. (0 km detour)
2. **Calangute Beach** – Popular beach. (0 km detour)
3. **Baga Beach** – Famous for nightlife. (0 km detour)
4. **Anjuna Beach & Flea Market** – Iconic beach and market. (0 km detour)
5. **Chapora Fort** – Scenic fort, Dil Chahta Hai fame. (~6 km detour from main road)
6. **Basilica of Bom Jesus** (Old Goa) – UNESCO World Heritage church. (0 km detour)
7. **Se Cathedral** (Old Goa) – Largest church in Asia. (0 km detour)

**South Goa:**
8. **Colva Beach** – Long sandy beach. (0 km detour)
9. **Palolem Beach** – Scenic crescent beach. (~36 km detour from Panaji)
10. **Cabo de Rama Fort** – Cliffside fort with views. (~25 km detour from main road)
11. **Agonda Beach** – Quiet, scenic beach. (~40 km detour from Panaji)

## Bengaluru to Madurai (Day 5: Dec 29)
- **Start Location:** Bengaluru
- **End Location:** Madurai, TN
- **Approx. Distance:** ≈435 km
- **Key Stops/Notes:** South India corridor, excellent highways, several tolls. Plan for 6–8 hours with breaks.

---
### Fastest Route (National Highways)
**Route:**
Bengaluru → Hosur (NH44) → Krishnagiri (NH44) → Dharmapuri (NH44) → Salem (NH44) → Karur (NH44) → Dindigul (NH44) → Madurai (NH44)

**Major Highway:** NH44 (entire stretch)

**Toll Plazas (approximate):**
- Bengaluru exit toll (NH44)
- Krishnagiri Toll Plaza (NH44)
- Dharmapuri Toll Plaza (NH44)
- Salem Toll Plaza (NH44)
- Karur Toll Plaza (NH44)
- Dindigul Toll Plaza (NH44)
- Madurai entry toll (NH44)

**Notes:**
This route is the fastest and most reliable, with excellent road conditions and facilities. Expect tolls at major highway sections. FASTag recommended.

---
### Tourist Attractions & Landmarks (En-route/Minor Detour)

1. **Hosur Hills** (Hosur) – Scenic views, local nature. (0 km detour)
2. **Krishnagiri Fort** (Krishnagiri) – Historic fort. (~5 km detour from NH44)
3. **Hogenakkal Falls** (near Dharmapuri) – Famous waterfalls. (~45 km detour from NH44 at Dharmapuri)
4. **Kottai Mariamman Temple** (Salem) – Noted temple. (0 km detour)
5. **Karur Pasupathieswarar Temple** (Karur) – Ancient Shiva temple. (0 km detour)
6. **Dindigul Fort** (Dindigul) – Historic fort. (~3 km detour from NH44)
7. **Meenakshi Amman Temple** (Madurai) – Iconic temple, city landmark. (0 km detour)
8. **Thirumalai Nayakkar Palace** (Madurai) – Historic palace. (0 km detour)
9. **Gandhi Memorial Museum** (Madurai) – Museum dedicated to Mahatma Gandhi. (0 km detour)

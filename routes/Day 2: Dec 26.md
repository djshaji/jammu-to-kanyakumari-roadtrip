## Gwalior to Nagpur (Day 2: Dec 26)
- **Start Location:** Gwalior
- **End Location:** Nagpur, MH
- **Approx. Distance:** ≈700 km
- **Key Stops/Notes:** Long drive. This is your second major haul.
- Tourist Stops: 
  - Jhansi Fort
  - Udaigiri Caves (quick detour near Vidisha)
  - Sanchi Stupa (UNESCO, near Vidisha)
  - Tropic of Cancer

---
### Fastest Route (National Highways)
**Route:**
Gwalior → Jhansi (NH44) → Sagar (NH44) → Narsinghpur (NH44) → Chhindwara (NH547) → Nagpur (NH547)

**Major Highways:** NH44, NH547

**Toll Plazas (approximate):**
- Gwalior-Jhansi Toll Plaza (NH44)
- Sagar Toll Plaza (NH44)
- Narsinghpur Toll Plaza (NH44)
- Chhindwara Toll Plaza (NH547)
- Nagpur entry toll (NH547)

**Notes:**
This route is the fastest and most reliable, with well-maintained roads and facilities. Expect tolls at major highway sections.

---
### Tourist Attractions & Landmarks (En-route/Minor Detour)

1. **Gwalior Fort** (Gwalior, start point) – Majestic hilltop fort, must-see. (0 km detour)
2. **Jhansi Fort** (Jhansi) – Historic fort, key site in 1857 uprising. (0 km detour)
3. **Datia Palace** (Datia, near Jhansi) – Stunning 7-storey palace. (~20 km detour from NH44)
4. **Lakshmi Narayan Temple** (Jhansi) – Noted temple, local heritage. (0 km detour)
5. **Udaigiri Caves** (near Vidisha) – Ancient rock-cut caves, Gupta era. (~8 km detour from NH44 at Vidisha)
6. **Sanchi Stupa** (near Vidisha) – UNESCO World Heritage Buddhist site. (~10 km detour from NH44 at Vidisha)
7. **Bada Ganesh Mandir** (Sagar) – Large Ganesh temple, local landmark. (0 km detour)
8. **Tropic of Cancer marker** (crosses NH44 near Sagar) – Geographic landmark. (0 km detour)
9. **Pench National Park** (near Nagpur) – Famous wildlife sanctuary. (~30 km detour from NH44 before Nagpur)
10. **Ramtek Temple** (near Nagpur) – Ancient hilltop temple. (~40 km detour from NH44 before Nagpur)
11. **Deekshabhoomi** (Nagpur) – Buddhist monument, major pilgrimage site. (0 km detour)

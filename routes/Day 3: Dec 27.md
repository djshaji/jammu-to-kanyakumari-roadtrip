## Nagpur to Hyderabad (Day 3: Dec 27)
- **Start Location:** Nagpur
- **End Location:** Hyderabad, TS
- **Approx. Distance:** ≈500 km
- **Key Stops/Notes:** Relatively shorter day. You could spend the evening/night near the city center.

---
### Fastest Route (National Highways)
**Route:**
Nagpur → Adilabad (NH44) → Nirmal → Kamareddy → Hyderabad (NH44)

**Major Highway:** NH44

**Toll Plazas (approximate):**
- Nagpur exit toll (NH44)
- Adilabad Toll Plaza (NH44)
- Nirmal Toll Plaza (NH44)
- Kamareddy Toll Plaza (NH44)
- Hyderabad entry toll (NH44)

**Notes:**
This route is the fastest and most reliable, with well-maintained roads and facilities. Expect tolls at major highway sections.

### Tourist Attractions & Landmarks (En-route/Minor Detour)

1. **Adilabad Fort** (Adilabad) – Historic fort, local heritage. (0 km detour)
2. **Kuntala Waterfall** (near Nirmal) – Tallest waterfall in Telangana. (~12 km detour from NH44 at Nirmal)
3. **Basar Saraswathi Temple** (Basar, near Nirmal) – Famous temple on Godavari river. (~35 km detour from NH44 at Nirmal)
4. **Nirmal Toys & Crafts** (Nirmal) – Traditional wooden toys, local market. (0 km detour)
5. **Pocharam Wildlife Sanctuary** (near Kamareddy) – Bird sanctuary, nature spot. (~20 km detour from NH44 at Kamareddy)
6. **Medak Cathedral** (Medak, near Kamareddy) – Largest church in Telangana. (~40 km detour from NH44 at Kamareddy)
7. **Hussain Sagar Lake** (Hyderabad) – Iconic lake with Buddha statue. (0 km detour)
8. **Charminar** (Hyderabad) – Historic monument, must-see. (0 km detour)
9. **Golconda Fort** (Hyderabad) – Majestic fort, evening sound & light show. (~12 km detour from NH44 in Hyderabad)
10. **Salar Jung Museum** (Hyderabad) – Renowned museum, art & history. (0 km detour)

## Madurai to Kanyakumari (Day 6: Dec 30)
- **Start Location:** Madurai
- **End Location:** Kanyakumari, TN
- **Approx. Distance:** ≈245 km
- **Key Stops/Notes:** Final stretch to the southern tip of India. Plan for 4–5 hours with breaks.

---
### Fastest Route (National Highways)
**Route:**
Madurai → Tirunelveli (NH44) → Nagercoil (NH44) → Kanyakumari (NH44)

**Major Highway:** NH44 (entire stretch)

**Toll Plazas (approximate):**
- Madurai exit toll (NH44)
- Tirunelveli Toll Plaza (NH44)
- Nagercoil Toll Plaza (NH44)
- Kanyakumari entry toll (NH44)

**Notes:**
This route is the fastest and most reliable, with excellent road conditions and facilities. Expect tolls at major highway sections. FASTag recommended.

---
### Tourist Attractions & Landmarks (En-route/Minor Detour)

1. **Thirumalai Nayakkar Palace** (Madurai) – Historic palace. (0 km detour)
2. **Meenakshi Amman Temple** (Madurai) – Iconic temple, city landmark. (0 km detour)
3. **Srivilliputhur Andal Temple** (Srivilliputhur, near NH44) – Ancient temple. (~10 km detour from NH44)
4. **Tirunelveli Nellaiappar Temple** (Tirunelveli) – Famous Shiva temple. (0 km detour)
5. **Manimuthar Falls** (near Tirunelveli) – Scenic waterfalls. (~35 km detour from NH44 at Tirunelveli)
6. **Padmanabhapuram Palace** (near Nagercoil) – Historic wooden palace. (~20 km detour from NH44 at Nagercoil)
7. **Vivekananda Rock Memorial** (Kanyakumari) – Iconic monument at the tip of India. (0 km detour)
8. **Thiruvalluvar Statue** (Kanyakumari) – Monument to Tamil poet. (0 km detour)
9. **Kanyakumari Beach & Sunset Point** (Kanyakumari) – Scenic views. (0 km detour)

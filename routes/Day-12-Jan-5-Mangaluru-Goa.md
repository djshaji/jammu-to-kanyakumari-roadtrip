## Mangaluru to Goa (Day 12: Jan 5)
- **Start Location:** Mangaluru, KA
- **End Location:** Goa
- **Approx. Distance:** ≈360 km
- **Key Stops/Notes:** Scenic coastal drive along NH66. Plan for 7–8 hours with stops.

---
### Fastest Route (National Highways)
**Route:**
Mangaluru → Udupi (NH66) → Kundapura (NH66) → Karwar (NH66) → Madgaon (NH66) → Panaji (NH66, Goa)

**Major Highway:** NH66 (entire stretch)

**Toll Plazas (approximate):**
- Mangaluru exit toll (NH66)
- Udupi Toll Plaza (NH66)
- Kundapura Toll Plaza (NH66)
- Karwar Toll Plaza (NH66)
- Goa entry toll (NH66)

**Notes:**
This route is the fastest and most reliable, with good road conditions and facilities. Expect tolls at major highway sections. FASTag recommended.

---
### Tourist Attractions & Landmarks (En-route/Minor Detour)

1. **St. Aloysius Chapel** (Mangaluru) – Famous for its frescoes. (0 km detour)
2. **Udupi Sri Krishna Temple** (Udupi) – Major pilgrimage site. (0 km detour)
3. **Malpe Beach** (near Udupi) – Popular beach. (~7 km detour from NH66)
4. **Kundapura Kodi Beach** (Kundapura) – Scenic beach. (~5 km detour from NH66)
5. **Murudeshwar Temple & Beach** (near Bhatkal) – Iconic Shiva statue, scenic beach. (~16 km detour from NH66)
6. **Gokarna Beach & Mahabaleshwar Temple** (Gokarna, near Karwar) – Famous temple and beach. (~10 km detour from NH66)
7. **Karwar Beach** (Karwar) – Scenic beach. (0 km detour)
8. **Dudhsagar Falls** (near Madgaon) – Famous waterfall. (~40 km detour from NH66)
9. **Basilica of Bom Jesus** (Old Goa) – UNESCO World Heritage church. (0 km detour)
10. **Fort Aguada** (near Panaji) – Historic fort. (~8 km detour from NH66)
11. **Calangute & Baga Beaches** (near Panaji) – Popular beaches. (~10 km detour from NH66)

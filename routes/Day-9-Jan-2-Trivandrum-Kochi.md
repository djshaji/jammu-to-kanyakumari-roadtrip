## Trivandrum to Kochi (Day 9: Jan 2)
- **Start Location:** Thiruvananthapuram (Trivandrum)
- **End Location:** Kochi, KL
- **Approx. Distance:** ≈200 km
- **Key Stops/Notes:** Scenic coastal drive via Kollam and Alleppey. Plan for 5–6 hours with stops.

---
### Fastest Route (National Highways)
**Route:**
Thiruvananthapuram → Kollam (NH66) → Alappuzha (Alleppey, NH66) → Kochi (NH66)

**Major Highway:** NH66 (entire stretch)

**Toll Plazas (approximate):**
- Kollam Toll Plaza (NH66)
- Alappuzha Toll Plaza (NH66)
- Kochi entry toll (NH66)

**Notes:**
This route is the fastest and most reliable, with good road conditions and facilities. Expect tolls at major highway sections. FASTag recommended.

---
### Tourist Attractions & Landmarks (En-route/Minor Detour)

1. **Kollam Beach & Lighthouse** (Kollam) – Scenic beach and historic lighthouse. (0 km detour)
2. **Thangassery Fort** (Kollam) – Ruins of a Portuguese fort. (~5 km detour from NH66)
3. **Ashtamudi Lake** (Kollam) – Famous backwater destination. (~8 km detour from NH66)
4. **Alappuzha Beach** (Alappuzha) – Popular beach. (0 km detour)
5. **Alappuzha Backwaters** (Alappuzha) – Houseboat cruises, scenic views. (0 km detour)
6. **Ambalappuzha Sri Krishna Temple** (near Alappuzha) – Ancient temple. (~14 km detour from NH66)
7. **Marari Beach** (near Alappuzha) – Quiet beach. (~12 km detour from NH66)
8. **Cherai Beach** (near Kochi) – Famous beach. (~25 km detour from NH66)
9. **Fort Kochi** (Kochi) – Historic area, Chinese fishing nets, colonial architecture. (0 km detour)
10. **Mattancherry Palace** (Kochi) – Portuguese palace, museum. (0 km detour)
11. **Jew Town & Synagogue** (Kochi) – Historic Jewish quarter. (0 km detour)

## Hyderabad to Bengaluru (Day 4: Dec 28)
- **Start Location:** Hyderabad
- **End Location:** Bengaluru, KA
- **Approx. Distance:** ≈570 km
- **Key Stops/Notes:** Major South India corridor, excellent highways, several tolls. Plan for 8–10 hours with breaks.

---
### Fastest Route (National Highways)
**Route:**
Hyderabad → Kurnool (NH44) → Anantapur (NH44) → Bengaluru (NH44)

**Major Highway:** NH44 (entire stretch)

**Toll Plazas (approximate):**
- Hyderabad exit toll (NH44)
- Kurnool Toll Plaza (NH44)
- Anantapur Toll Plaza (NH44)
- Bengaluru entry toll (NH44)

**Notes:**
This route is the fastest and most reliable, with excellent road conditions and facilities. Expect tolls at major highway sections. FASTag recommended.

---
### Tourist Attractions & Landmarks (En-route/Minor Detour)

1. **Kurnool Fort** (Kurnool) – Historic fort, local heritage. (0 km detour)
2. **Belum Caves** (near Kurnool) – Largest caves in South India. (~30 km detour from NH44 at Kurnool)
3. **Lepakshi Temple** (near Anantapur) – Famous for its hanging pillar and murals. (~15 km detour from NH44 at Anantapur)
4. **Penukonda Fort** (Penukonda, near Anantapur) – Ancient fort. (~20 km detour from NH44 at Anantapur)
5. **Nandi Hills** (near Bengaluru) – Scenic hill station. (~25 km detour from NH44 before Bengaluru)
6. **ISKCON Temple** (Bengaluru) – Major spiritual center. (0 km detour)
7. **Bangalore Palace** (Bengaluru) – Historic palace, city landmark. (0 km detour)
8. **Cubbon Park** (Bengaluru) – Iconic city park. (0 km detour)

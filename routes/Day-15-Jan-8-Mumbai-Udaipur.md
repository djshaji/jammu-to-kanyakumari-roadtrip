## Mumbai to Udaipur (Day 15: Jan 8)
- **Start Location:** Mumbai, MH
- **End Location:** Udaipur, RJ
- **Approx. Distance:** ≈760 km
- **Key Stops/Notes:** Long drive through Maharashtra and Rajasthan. Plan for 12–14 hours with breaks.

---
### Fastest Route (National Highways)
**Route:**
Mumbai → Surat (NH48) → Vadodara (NH48) → Ahmedabad (NH48) → Udaipur (NH48/NH27)

**Major Highways:** NH48, NH27

**Toll Plazas (approximate):**
- Mumbai exit toll (NH48)
- Surat Toll Plaza (NH48)
- Vadodara Toll Plaza (NH48)
- Ahmedabad Toll Plaza (NH48)
- Udaipur entry toll (NH27)

**Notes:**
This route is the fastest and most reliable, with excellent road conditions and facilities. Expect tolls at major highway sections. FASTag recommended.

---
### Tourist Attractions & Landmarks (En-route/Minor Detour)

1. **Gateway of India** (Mumbai) – Iconic monument. (0 km detour)
2. **Marine Drive & Chowpatty Beach** (Mumbai) – Famous promenade and beach. (0 km detour)
3. **Surat Castle** (Surat) – Historic fort. (~6 km detour from NH48)
4. **Dutch Garden** (Surat) – Colonial-era garden. (~5 km detour from NH48)
5. **Laxmi Vilas Palace** (Vadodara) – Grand palace. (~7 km detour from NH48)
6. **Sayaji Baug (Kamati Baug)** (Vadodara) – Large public park. (~4 km detour from NH48)
7. **Sabarmati Ashram** (Ahmedabad) – Gandhi's residence. (~8 km detour from NH48)
8. **Adalaj Stepwell** (near Ahmedabad) – Historic stepwell. (~18 km detour from NH48)
9. **City Palace** (Udaipur) – Iconic palace complex. (0 km detour)
10. **Lake Pichola & Jag Mandir** (Udaipur) – Scenic lake and palace. (0 km detour)
11. **Jagdish Temple** (Udaipur) – Historic temple. (0 km detour)
12. **Saheliyon-ki-Bari** (Udaipur) – Famous garden. (0 km detour)

## Udaipur to Delhi (Day 16: Jan 9)
- **Start Location:** Udaipur, RJ
- **End Location:** Delhi
- **Approx. Distance:** ≈670 km
- **Key Stops/Notes:** Long drive through Rajasthan and Haryana. Plan for 10–12 hours with breaks.

---
### Fastest Route (National Highways)
**Route:**
Udaipur → Chittorgarh (NH48) → Bhilwara (NH48) → Ajmer (NH48) → Jaipur (NH48) → Gurugram (NH48) → Delhi (NH48)

**Major Highway:** NH48 (entire stretch)

**Toll Plazas (approximate):**
- Udaipur exit toll (NH48)
- Chittorgarh Toll Plaza (NH48)
- Bhilwara Toll Plaza (NH48)
- Ajmer Toll Plaza (NH48)
- Jaipur Toll Plaza (NH48)
- Gurugram Toll Plaza (NH48)
- Delhi entry toll (NH48)

**Notes:**
This route is the fastest and most reliable, with excellent road conditions and facilities. Expect tolls at major highway sections. FASTag recommended.

---
### Tourist Attractions & Landmarks (En-route/Minor Detour)

1. **City Palace** (Udaipur) – Iconic palace complex. (0 km detour)
2. **Lake Pichola & Jag Mandir** (Udaipur) – Scenic lake and palace. (0 km detour)
3. **Chittorgarh Fort** (Chittorgarh) – Largest fort in India. (~7 km detour from NH48)
4. **Ajmer Sharif Dargah** (Ajmer) – Famous Sufi shrine. (~5 km detour from NH48)
5. **Pushkar Lake & Brahma Temple** (Pushkar, near Ajmer) – Sacred lake and temple. (~15 km detour from NH48)
6. **Amber Fort** (Jaipur) – Majestic hilltop fort. (~12 km detour from NH48)
7. **Hawa Mahal** (Jaipur) – Iconic palace. (0 km detour)
8. **Jantar Mantar** (Jaipur) – UNESCO World Heritage observatory. (0 km detour)
9. **Kingdom of Dreams** (Gurugram) – Entertainment complex. (~6 km detour from NH48)
10. **Qutub Minar** (Delhi) – UNESCO World Heritage site. (0 km detour)
11. **India Gate** (Delhi) – Iconic war memorial. (0 km detour)
12. **Red Fort** (Delhi) – UNESCO World Heritage fort. (0 km detour)

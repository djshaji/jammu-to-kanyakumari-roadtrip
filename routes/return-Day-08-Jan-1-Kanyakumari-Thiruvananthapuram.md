## Kanyakumari to Thiruvananthapuram (Day 8: Jan 1)
- **Start Location:** Kanyakumari
- **End Location:** Thiruvananthapuram (Trivandrum), KL
- **Approx. Distance:** ≈90 km
- **Key Stops/Notes:** Short coastal drive, scenic views, easy highway. Plan for 2–3 hours with stops.

---
### Fastest Route (National Highways)
**Route:**
Kanyakumari → Nagercoil (NH66) → Thiruvananthapuram (NH66)

**Major Highway:** NH66 (entire stretch)

**Toll Plazas (approximate):**
- Nagercoil Toll Plaza (NH66)
- Thiruvananthapuram entry toll (NH66)

**Notes:**
This route is the fastest and most reliable, with good road conditions and facilities. Expect tolls at major highway sections. FASTag recommended.

---
### Tourist Attractions & Landmarks (En-route/Minor Detour)

1. **Vivekananda Rock Memorial** (Kanyakumari) – Iconic monument at the tip of India. (0 km detour)
2. **Thiruvalluvar Statue** (Kanyakumari) – Monument to Tamil poet. (0 km detour)
3. **Kanyakumari Beach & Sunset Point** (Kanyakumari) – Scenic views. (0 km detour)
4. **Padmanabhapuram Palace** (near Nagercoil) – Historic wooden palace. (~20 km detour from NH66 at Nagercoil)
5. **Suchindram Temple** (Suchindram, near Nagercoil) – Ancient temple. (~7 km detour from NH66)
6. **Poovar Backwaters** (near Thiruvananthapuram) – Scenic backwater destination. (~15 km detour from NH66 before Thiruvananthapuram)
7. **Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple** (Thiruvananthapuram) – Iconic temple, city landmark. (0 km detour)
8. **Napier Museum & Zoo** (Thiruvananthapuram) – Heritage museum and zoological park. (0 km detour)
9. **Kovalam Beach** (near Thiruvananthapuram) – Famous beach. (~12 km detour from NH66)
